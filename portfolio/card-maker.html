card-maker

Design and development for the new version for card customization website
UI UX digital

oh - ver - vyoo

We want to completely reconstruct the format of the design and switch over to an application format. We will organize each category for specific occasions so the user can easily click which one they desire. We will get give them a step by step design process to change text, font, background, and insert images into their greeting card to make it completely unique. At the end of their user journey, they will be able to send a physical or electronic card to whoever they wish. They will also be able to share their design on Facebook and twitter to share their unique design as well as promote our product.
